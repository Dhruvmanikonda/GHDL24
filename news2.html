The GHDL 2023/2024 season had numerous key events that changed the final outcome of the tournament. DPS Khajaguda, the finalists of the premier season narrowly missed out on the title in the final. However, they would have never even qualified for the knockout stages if not for the withdrawal of Hyderabad Little Stars from the tournament. Another key event in this thrilling season was the sole penalty shootout of the tournament in the semi final between USFC and DPS Khajaguda, a game which DPS Khajaguda won 3-2 on penalties. In spite of that, the best moment of the tournament came in the finals when Arjit Gandhi scored as a goalkeeper from his own box to equalize the score against DPS Khajaguda.
